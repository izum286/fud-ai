Features

Everything you need to take control of your nutrition

AI Food Recognition

  • Snap a photo of any meal for instant estimates
  • Scan nutrition labels for precise data
  • Analyze photos from your camera roll
  • Type food name and quantity for AI lookup
  • Voice input — speak your meal, no typing needed

Nutrition Tracking

  • Track 13 nutrients per entry
  • Adjustable serving sizes with live recalculation
  • Organize by meal type
  • Browse past days with date selector

Smart Dashboard

  • Daily calorie display with progress bar
  • Macro cards with gradient visuals
  • Detailed nutrition breakdown view
  • Food log grouped by meal with swipe-to-delete

Progress & Analytics

  • Weight chart with trend and goal weight line
  • Goal-reached detection and celebration
  • Calorie trend chart: intake vs. goal
  • Macro averages with week and month filters

Personalized Plans

  • Onboarding collects your body stats and goals
  • BMR/TDEE calculated automatically
  • Auto daily targets for calories and macros
  • Fully customizable — override any value

Apple Health

  • Writes body mass, height, and body fat %
  • Reads weight, height, body fat, DOB, and sex
  • Bidirectional sync — auto profile updates
  • Background observer for real-time sync

iCloud Sync

  • Entries sync to your private iCloud database
  • Restore all data on a new device
  • Automatic sync on every add and delete
  • Sign in with Apple for secure auth

Smart Notifications

  • Daily summary: consumed vs. remaining
  • Never miss logging a meal
  • Set your preferred reminder times
