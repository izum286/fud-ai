§ 02   Chapters

What it does, chapter by chapter.

01

Capture a meal.

Photo · Voice · Text · Label

Snap a photo of any meal for instant estimates, scan a nutrition label for precise per-serving data, speak your lunch hands-free, or just type it.

  • Native camera + Photos library import
  • Voice input via 5 speech-to-text providers
  • Text fallback for quick logging
02

AI does the math.

13 LLM providers

Bring your own key. Choose from Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Grok, OpenRouter, Together AI, Groq, Hugging Face, Fireworks, DeepInfra, Mistral, Ollama (local), or any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

  • Open-weight vision models out of the box: Gemma, Llama Vision, Qwen VL, Pixtral
  • API keys stored in iOS Keychain — never transmitted to us
  • Switch providers and models anytime
03

Track 13 nutrients.

Macros + micros

Calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, mono-unsaturated fat, poly-unsaturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium, and added sugar — adjustable serving sizes recalculate everything live.

  • Personalized BMR + TDEE via Katch-McArdle / Mifflin-St Jeor
  • Auto daily targets, fully overridable
  • Browse past days with the date strip
04

Talk to your Coach.

Multi-turn AI chat

A dedicated tab where the AI sees your full profile, weight history, food log, and a thermodynamic forecast. Ask "what's my expected weight in 30 days?" or "am I eating enough protein?" and get answers grounded in your own data.

  • Memory persists locally across sessions
  • Goal-aware prompt chips for Lose / Gain / Maintain
  • Long-press any reply to copy
05

Glance from anywhere.

Home + Lock Screen widgets

Five widget families across Home Screen and Lock Screen — circular, rectangular, inline, small, medium. Live-update the moment you log a meal. Auto-roll over at midnight without opening the app.

  • Calorie ring + macro progress at a glance
  • Above-the-clock circular and below-the-clock rectangular
  • Inline single-line text for the Lock Screen header
06

Sync with Apple Health.

Bidirectional · 12 nutrition types

Writes 12 nutrition types per meal plus body measurements (weight, height, body fat). Reads weight, height, body fat, date of birth, and sex back into your profile automatically.

  • Background observers for real-time bi-sync
  • UUID-tagged samples for safe per-entry deletion
  • Delete All Data is local-only — Apple Health is never touched
07

Speaks fifteen languages.

Auto-localized

English, Arabic, Azerbaijani, Chinese, Dutch, French, German, Hindi, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Spanish — auto-selected by your iPhone's system language. No in-app picker needed.

§ 03   Process

From photo to log in seconds.

Step 01

Capture

Snap a photo, scan a label, pick from library, type, or speak your food details.

Step 02

Analyze

Your chosen AI provider identifies the food and estimates the full nutrition profile.

Step 03

Review

Edit the name, adjust serving size, confirm meal type. You stay in control of every entry.

Step 04

Log

Saved locally on your device. Synced to Apple Health if enabled. Widgets update instantly.

Step 05

Track

Dashboard, charts, and Coach context update in real time across the app.

Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets. Apple Health bidirectional sync. 15 languages. Privacy-first, no account, no cloud sync, BYOK. MIT licensed.
