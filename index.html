Fud AI - Free AI Calorie Tracker App for iPhone

Fud AI is the best free calorie tracker app for iPhone and iOS. Use artificial intelligence to scan food, count calories, and track nutrition instantly. Take a photo of your meal and get a complete nutrition breakdown with calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, and potassium.

AI Food Scanner and Nutrition Label Reader

Fud AI uses AI food recognition to identify meals from photos. Scan nutrition labels for accurate per-serving data. Voice input lets you speak your meals for hands-free calorie tracking via 5 speech-to-text providers: Native iOS, OpenAI Whisper, Groq, Deepgram, and AssemblyAI. Text input for quick food logging. Works with 13 AI providers: Google Gemini, OpenAI GPT-5, Anthropic Claude Sonnet 4.6, xAI Grok, Groq, Together AI, OpenRouter, Hugging Face, Fireworks AI, DeepInfra, Mistral Pixtral, Ollama for local AI inference, and any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint. Supports open-weight vision models including Google Gemma, Meta Llama Vision, Qwen VL, and Mistral Pixtral.

AI Coach — Personalized Weight & Nutrition Chat

Multi-turn AI Coach chat has full context of your profile, weight history, food log, and computed forecast. Ask questions like "what's my expected weight in 30 days?", "am I eating enough protein?", or "what should I eat for dinner?". Coach uses your active BMR formula (Katch-McArdle when body fat is known, Mifflin-St Jeor otherwise) and thermodynamic weight forecast to ground its answers in your actual data. Memory persists across sessions; reset anytime.

Home Screen and Lock Screen Widgets

iOS widgets show today's calorie ring and macro bars at a glance. Home Screen widgets come in small (ring + kcal remaining) and medium (ring + P/C/F bars). Lock Screen widgets include circular (above-the-clock), rectangular (below-the-clock), and inline. Widgets update the instant you log or delete a meal — no tap-to-open required. Auto-rolls over at midnight.

Macro Tracker and TDEE Calculator

Track macros including protein, carbs, and fat. Calculate your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) and BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) using Katch-McArdle and Mifflin-St Jeor formulas. Set personalized calorie and macro goals. Weight tracking with goal weight and trend visualization. Progress charts with 1 week, 1 month, 3 month, 6 month, 1 year, and all time views.

15 Languages — Auto Localized

Ships in 15 languages auto-selected from your iPhone's system language: English, Arabic, Azerbaijani, Dutch, French, German, Hindi, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese (Brazil), Romanian, Russian, Simplified Chinese, and Spanish. No in-app picker — matches what iOS already knows.

Privacy First Calorie Counter

No account needed. No sign in required. No cloud sync. No ads. No analytics. No tracking. All data stored locally on your device. API keys encrypted in iOS Keychain. Delete All Data is local-only — it never touches Apple Health, preserving your personal health history. Open source under MIT License. Bring your own API key (BYOK). Free calorie counter app. Best alternative to MyFitnessPal, Lose It, Cronometer, MacroFactor, and Carbon Diet Coach.

Apple Health Integration

Bidirectional sync with Apple Health. Writes 12 nutrition types per meal (calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, monounsaturated fat, polyunsaturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium) and body measurements (weight, height, body fat percentage). Reads weight, height, body fat, date of birth, and biological sex. Background health data observer. Compatible with Apple Watch health data. Works on iOS 17.6 and later.
