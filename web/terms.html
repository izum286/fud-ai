Terms of Service

Last updated: April 25, 2026

By downloading, installing, or using Fud AI ("the app"), on either iOS or Android, you agree to these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

1. Description of Service

Fud AI is a free, open-source nutrition tracking application available on iOS and Android. It uses AI to estimate the nutritional content of food from photos, nutrition labels (iOS), text descriptions, voice input, and manual entry. It also includes an AI Coach chat for personalized weight and nutrition guidance, Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets (iOS) / Glance widgets (Android), bidirectional sync with Apple Health (iOS) and Health Connect (Android), and localization in 15 languages auto-selected by your phone's system language. The app supports 13 AI providers and 5 speech-to-text providers — you bring your own API key for whichever you choose.

2. AI-Powered Estimates

Nutritional information, weight forecasts, and Coach responses provided by Fud AI are generated by your chosen AI provider and are estimated, not exact. These estimates should be used as general guidance only. Fud AI is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for dietary or weight-related decisions.

3. AI Coach Chat Disclaimer

The AI Coach tab is a conversational assistant that reads your profile, weight log, and food log to produce suggestions. Its replies are generated by the third-party AI provider you select and may be inaccurate, biased, or outdated. Coach is not a substitute for advice from a dietitian, doctor, or other qualified professional. Do not rely on Coach for decisions about medical conditions, eating disorders, pregnancy, or any other health matter that warrants professional guidance.

4. Your API Keys

  • You are responsible for obtaining and managing your own API keys from your chosen AI and speech-to-text providers.
  • API keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us — in iOS Keychain on iOS, in EncryptedSharedPreferences using AES-256 (Android Keystore-backed) on Android.
  • Usage, rate limits, and billing for AI/STT requests are between you and the provider you chose.
  • We do not proxy, route, store, or have any access to your API keys or the payloads they authenticate.

5. Third-Party Providers

When you use the app with an AI or STT provider, your requests (including food photos, voice audio, and Coach messages) are sent directly from your device to that provider. Each provider has its own terms and privacy policy that govern how they handle that data. You are responsible for reviewing and accepting those providers' terms. We do not control, endorse, or make representations about third-party providers.

6. Open Source

Fud AI is open source under the MIT License. You may use, modify, and distribute the source code in accordance with the license terms. The full source — for both iOS (SwiftUI) and Android (Kotlin + Jetpack Compose), including every network call the app makes — is at github.com/apoorvdarshan/fud-ai.

7. Health Disclaimer

Fud AI is a nutrition tracking tool, not a medical application. The app does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Nutritional estimates, BMR/TDEE values, macro targets, and weight forecasts are approximations based on published formulas (Katch-McArdle, Mifflin-St Jeor) and energy-balance math (7700 kcal ≈ 1 kg); they may not be accurate for all individuals. Users with medical conditions, eating disorders, or specific dietary needs should consult qualified healthcare professionals.

8. Apple Health & Health Connect Integration

By enabling Apple Health (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) integration, you authorize the app to read and write health data as described in our Privacy Policy. Written data includes body measurements (weight, height, body fat percentage) and per-meal nutrition (12 types: calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, monounsaturated fat, polyunsaturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium). On iOS, you can revoke access via Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Fud AI; on Android, via the Health Connect app. Note that Delete All Data in the app only wipes local storage — your Apple Health / Health Connect samples persist unless you remove them separately.

10. Availability

The app requires an internet connection for AI food analysis, Coach chat, and remote speech-to-text. Fully offline operation is possible only when you use Ollama (local LLM) and the on-device speech recognizer (Native iOS or Native Android). Offline functionality otherwise is limited to viewing previously logged data, running local BMR/TDEE math, manual entry, reading from existing Apple Health / Health Connect samples, and re-logging from Saved Meals. Some Android OEM skins (OriginOS, MIUI/HyperOS, One UI) aggressively kill background alarms; the app exposes a Battery Optimization deep-link in Settings to whitelist Fud AI so scheduled reminders fire reliably — without whitelisting, reminders may be silently delayed or dropped.
