§ 02   Chapters

What it does, chapter by chapter.

01

Capture a meal.

Photo · Voice · Text · Manual

Snap a photo of any meal for instant estimates, scan a nutrition label for precise per-serving data (iOS), speak your lunch hands-free, type it, or enter macros manually when you already know them.

  • Native camera + photo library import on both platforms
  • Voice input via 5 speech-to-text providers
  • Text and manual fallbacks for quick logging
02

AI does the math.

13 LLM providers

Bring your own key. Choose from Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Grok, OpenRouter, Together AI, Groq, Hugging Face, Fireworks, DeepInfra, Mistral, Ollama (local), or any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

  • Open-weight vision models out of the box: Gemma, Llama Vision, Qwen VL, Pixtral
  • API keys stored in iOS Keychain (iOS) or EncryptedSharedPreferences with AES-256 (Android) — never transmitted to us
  • Switch providers and models anytime; transient overloads (503 / 429 / 529) auto-retry with backoff
03

Track 13 nutrients.

Macros + micros

Calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, mono-unsaturated fat, poly-unsaturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium, and added sugar — adjustable serving sizes recalculate everything live.

  • Personalized BMR + TDEE via Katch-McArdle / Mifflin-St Jeor
  • Auto daily targets, fully overridable, with per-macro pin / auto-balance
  • Browse past days with the date strip; configurable week start
04

Talk to your Coach.

Multi-turn AI chat

A dedicated tab where the AI sees your full profile, weight history, food log, and a thermodynamic forecast. Ask "what's my expected weight in 30 days?" or "am I eating enough protein?" and get answers grounded in your own data.

  • Memory persists locally across sessions
  • Goal-aware prompt chips for Lose / Gain / Maintain
  • Long-press any reply to copy
05

Glance from anywhere.

Home Screen + Lock Screen widgets

iOS ships five widget families across Home Screen and Lock Screen — circular, rectangular, inline, small, medium — with both Calorie and Protein widgets. Android ships Calorie and Protein Glance widgets in two sizes each (small + medium). All update the moment you log a meal.

  • Pink-gradient calorie ring with macro progress at a glance
  • Above-the-clock circular and below-the-clock rectangular on iOS Lock Screen
  • Auto-roll over at midnight without opening the app
06

Sync with Apple Health & Health Connect.

Bidirectional · iOS + Android

iOS writes 12 nutrition types per meal plus body measurements (weight, height, body fat) to Apple Health and reads weight, height, body fat, date of birth, and sex back into your profile. Android does the same through Health Connect — also pulling in external weight samples from Samsung Health, Fitbit, Withings, and any other Health Connect–compatible app.

  • Background observers for real-time bi-sync
  • UUID-tagged samples for safe per-entry deletion (no orphans)
  • Delete All Data is local-only — Apple Health / Health Connect data is never touched
07

Speaks fifteen languages.

Auto-localized

English, Arabic, Azerbaijani, Chinese (Simplified), Dutch, French, German, Hindi, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese (Brazil), Romanian, Russian, Spanish — auto-selected by your phone's system language on both iOS and Android. No in-app picker needed.

§ 03   Process

From photo to log in seconds.

Step 01

Capture

Snap a photo, scan a label, pick from library, type, or speak your food details.

Step 02

Analyze

Your chosen AI provider identifies the food and estimates the full nutrition profile.

Step 03

Review

Edit the name, adjust serving size, confirm meal type. You stay in control of every entry.

Step 04

Log

Saved locally on your device. Synced to Apple Health or Health Connect if enabled. Widgets update instantly.

Step 05

Track

Dashboard, charts, and Coach context update in real time across the app.

Fud AI — Free AI Calorie Tracker App for iPhone & Android

Free open-source calorie tracker for iPhone and Android. AI food recognition from photos. Scan nutrition labels (iOS), voice input, text logging, manual entry. Supports 13 AI providers: Google Gemini, OpenAI GPT-5, Anthropic Claude Sonnet 4.6 / Opus 4.7 / Haiku 4.5, xAI Grok, Groq, Together AI, OpenRouter, Hugging Face, Fireworks AI, DeepInfra, Mistral Pixtral, Ollama for local AI. Tracks 13 nutrients including calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, mono-fat, poly-fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium. AI Coach chat with weight forecast. Calorie + Protein widgets — Home Screen and Lock Screen on iOS, Glance on Android. Apple Health (iOS) and Health Connect (Android) bidirectional sync. 15 languages auto-selected by phone's system language. Privacy-first, no account, no cloud sync, BYOK. MIT licensed. Built with SwiftUI on iOS, Jetpack Compose on Android. Best alternative to MyFitnessPal, Lose It, Cronometer, MacroFactor, Carbon Diet Coach, Cal AI, Yazio.
